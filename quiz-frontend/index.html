ℹ️ About & How to Play

🌟 Welcome to the Bible Quiz Review Game!

This interactive, classroom-style review game is designed to help church kids review Bible stories, characters, and verses. Built with custom audio synthesis, rolling animations, hidden power-ups, and robust template utilities, it is the perfect tool for classroom engagement, Sunday schools, and youth groups!

🎮 Gameplay & Rules

  • Team Setup: Configure custom names, upload custom logos, or choose cartoon animal avatars in the sidebar Control Center.
  • Turn Selection: Teams take turns selecting question tiles from the interactive game board. The points value of each card is displayed on its bottom-right corner ribbon.
  • Score Math: Answering correctly adds the full points value to the team's score. Wrong answers subtract half the question value (unless disabled in Settings).
  • Steal System: If the active team gets it wrong or chooses to pass, the opposing team can attempt a steal. Correct steals award the points, while incorrect steals incur the standard wrong answer penalty.
  • End Game: Use the End button in the top control bar to instantly conclude the active game and show final standings.
  • Tiebreaker: If scores are tied at the end, a designated tiebreaker question automatically resolves the winner.

🎁 Hidden Power-ups

Special modifiers are randomly hidden beneath question cards. Revealing a power-up applies immediate benefits:

  • 🌟 Double Points: Doubles the point value of the question, giving you a chance to leap ahead!
  • 🛡️ Steal Shield: Protects your score! If you get the question wrong, the other team cannot steal the points.
  • 🔄 Second Chance: Gives your team a free second attempt if your first answer is incorrect.
  • 🩹 Safety Net: If you answer wrong, you lose 0 points instead of the normal 50% point deduction.

🛠️ Teacher Customization Suite

  • Bible Story Presets: Instantly load custom 12-question quiz presets (+1 tiebreaker each) like Noah's Ark, David & Goliath, Miracles of Jesus, Moses, Joseph, Daniel, or Jonah.
  • Interactive Editor: Customize questions, answers, MCQ options, points, and power-up types inside the Admin Panel. Assigning any custom power-up to a question automatically switches the game's power-up assignment mode to **Manual** so they are guaranteed to appear.
  • Format Selector (Save DB): Choose between **JSON (Full Backup)** to save the entire database (including category labels, custom teams, and settings) and **CSV (Excel)** to download questions and settings in a spreadsheet-compatible format.
  • Aesthetic Controls: Tailor the board appearance with custom fonts, grid colors, and tile designs. Toggle High Contrast mode for enhanced legibility.

🏠 Game Modes & Local Persistence

  • Practice Mode: Enable solo-learning mode on the dashboard to review questions independently without teams, score penalties, or turn limits.
  • Local Storage Protection: All scores, active game states, and custom settings/questions are saved in the browser's local storage. A mid-game reload or power outage will never lose your quiz progress!
  • Hall of Fame: Displays past winning teams, their scores, and correct answer statistics on the dashboard.
